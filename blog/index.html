April 2017

Laura, Wyatt, Uncle David, Lee, Old Grandma, and myself went on a 7-day cruise to the Western Caribbean! Belize: We visit Xunantunich, a beautiful Maya site. We are even permitted to climb to the top of El Castil(above)! The steps are so large that you need to use your hands to climb them and they wrap around the back of El Castil, so that one would seem to disappear and then reappear on top. The accoustics of the site are such that even speaking at a normal volume one can be clearly heard by those below.

October 2016

In celebration of Halloween, I ventured out to some of the top Haunts in the Area. The Bates Motel, Terror Behind the Walls, Sleepy Hollow. Pennhurst Asylum: After circling the area for 15mins, I manage to find the property nestled deep in the woods. After working my way through the Asylum's upper floors, I wait at the top of the basement stairs. We're led down into complete darkness. As the head of our group, I inch us forward along the passage, but the monsters jumping out the darkness always take my by suprise!

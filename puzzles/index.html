Talk Like A Pirate

Ye can always pretend to be a bloodthirsty , threatening everyone by waving yer sword in the air, but until ye learn to like a pirate, ye'll never be accepted as an authentic . So here's what ye do: Cleverly work into yer daily converstions pirate phrases such as "Ahoy there, ," "Avast, ye ," amd "Shiver me ." Remember to drop all yer g's when ye say such words as sailin', spittin', and fightin'. This will give ye a/an start to being recognized as a swashbucklin' . Once ye have the lingo down pat, it helps to wear a three-cornered on yer head, stash a/an in yer pants, and keep a/an perched atop yer . Aye, now ye be a real pirate!
